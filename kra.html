KRA PIN Application – Fast, Easy, and Essential

Are you a student with a Kenyan National ID? Then you need a KRA PIN—and we're here to make it simple for you!

Why is a KRA PIN Important?

A KRA PIN (Personal Identification Number) is required by law in Kenya for a wide range of essential services. As a student, having a KRA PIN is not just helpful—it's a must-have for:

HELB Applications

Required for all Higher Education Loans Board applications and disbursements

Banking Services

Necessary for opening bank accounts and financial transactions

Government Services

Essential for accessing all online government platforms

Employment

Required for formal employment, internships, and NHIF/NSSF registration

Pro Tip: If you've just received your National ID, it's time to apply for your KRA PIN—the sooner, the better.
