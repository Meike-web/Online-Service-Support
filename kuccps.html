KUCCPS Transfers – Fast, Easy & Guided

Need to change your university or course? We simplify the KUCCPS transfer process for you — from start to finish.

What is a KUCCPS Transfer?

A KUCCPS transfer allows you to:

  • Move from one university or college to another (inter-institutional transfer)
  • Change from one course to another (intra-institutional transfer)

It's your right as a student to request a transfer — and we're here to help make it successful.

What We Help You With

Portal Assistance

  • Accessing the KUCCPS portal
  • Step-by-step application assistance
  • Understanding cut-off points and eligibility

Application Support

  • Uploading and submitting your application correctly
  • Writing a clear reason for the transfer
  • Monitoring progress until completion

Who is Eligible?

You can request a transfer if:

Placement Conditions

You've been placed by KUCCPS but want to move to a different institution or course

Academic Requirements

You meet the required cut-off points for your preferred course

Availability

The institution you're transferring to has space available

Whether you're a 2024 KCSE candidate or from an earlier year, we can guide you.

Important Notes

  • One application per cycle: You can only apply once per cycle, so it must be done correctly.
  • Limited windows: Transfer windows are open for a limited time, usually announced by KUCCPS.
  • Transfer reason: You may be required to write a reason for transfer – we'll help you craft it properly.
  • Dual approval: Transfer approval requires both the receiving and releasing institution to confirm.
