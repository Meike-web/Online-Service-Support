Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

At MeikeHim, your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us or request our services, we may collect:

  • Your name, ID number, phone number, institution details, and supporting documents.
  • Any messages or communications you send through WhatsApp, SMS, or email.

We collect only the data necessary to complete your requested application services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To help you apply for KRA PINs, HELB loans and scholarships, or KUCCPS transfers.
  • To communicate with you regarding your application status.
  • To verify your documents and ensure all required details are submitted correctly.

We do not use your data for marketing or share it with any third parties.

3. Data Protection

  • Your information is kept confidential and stored securely during the application process.
  • After your application is successfully completed, we do not keep your documents or personal data.
  • We will never sell or disclose your personal information.

4. Communication

We may contact you via:

  • WhatsApp
  • Phone call
  • SMS

Only to follow up or update you regarding your application.

5. Consent

By using our website or services, you consent to this privacy policy.
