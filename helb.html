HELB & Scholarship Application Services

Trusted, Simple & Fast financial support for your education

Getting financial support for your education doesn't have to be complicated. At MeikeHim, we guide you step-by-step through both HELB Loan and Government Scholarship applications — so you can focus on your studies, not paperwork.

Whether you're applying for the first time or need help figuring out your eligibility, we're here for you.

What We Help You Apply For

1. HELB Loan

We assist with:

  • First-Time Undergraduate & TVET loan applications
  • Proper form filling and portal guidance
  • Uploading required documents
  • Printing and submission assistance

2. Government Scholarships

We support students in public universities and colleges in applying for government scholarships through the Funding Portal.

Important Application Guidelines

Public University Students:

Eligible for both HELB and Scholarship.

Private University Students:

Eligible for HELB only — scholarships are not available.

Diploma Students in Universities:

Not eligible for both HELB and SCHOLASHIP.
(Helb & Scholarships do not apply to diploma programs under universities.)

Are You Under 18 Without a National ID?

Good news — you can still apply! Here's how:

  • Open a Junior Bank Account under your parent or guardian's name
  • We'll guide you through every step of the process, even without an ID
